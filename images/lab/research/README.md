# RICG Home Research Images

Place one real image for each research direction in this directory. Keep the filenames exactly as listed below; the Home page already refers to these paths.

| File | Suggested subject | Preferred crop |
|---|---|---|
| `robot-control.jpg` | Robot control experiment, humanoid/arm motion control, or a controller-in-the-loop laboratory scene | 16:10 or 4:3 landscape |
| `embodied-learning.jpg` | Teleoperation, data collection, multimodal perception, or a VLA/learning workflow image | 16:10 or 4:3 landscape |
| `bimanual-manipulation.jpg` | Dual-arm transport, assembly, folding, or coordinated grasping | 16:10 or 4:3 landscape |
| `contact-rich-control.jpg` | Insertion, surface contact, force sensing, compliant manipulation, or physical interaction | 16:10 or 4:3 landscape |

Recommended resolution: at least `1400 × 900 px`.

The page uses `object-fit: cover`; keep the robot/task subject near the center so it survives cropping. When a file is absent, the card displays a textual fallback rather than a broken image.
