# RICG News Images

News images are stored under `images/lab/news/`. The Home-page Latest News section and the full `/news/` page share these files.

```text
whole-body-mpc.jpg
force-admittance-aircraft.jpg
cooperative-manipulation.png
bimanual-teleoperation-patent.png
placeholder.svg
```

Use landscape JPG or PNG files, ideally at least `1200 × 900 px` (4:3). The page uses `object-fit: cover`; keep the principal robot, experiment setup, or figure near the image center.

Until a matching image is supplied, the corresponding entry falls back to `placeholder.svg`.
