Who we are looking for

We are interested in applicants with backgrounds in robotics, control, mechanical engineering, computer science, artificial intelligence, optimization, vision, or related fields. Prior experience with robotic systems, software engineering, simulation, or learning-based methods is valuable but not required in every case.

Research opportunities

  • Ph.D. students pursuing foundational and deployment-oriented robot intelligence research.
  • Master students conducting research in robot control, manipulation, learning, and perception.
  • Research assistants and interns interested in hands-on robot software and experimentation.
  • Academic and industrial collaborators working on intelligent robot control and applications.

Application materials
